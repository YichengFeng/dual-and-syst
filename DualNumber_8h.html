Detailed Description

Single-variable dual number for first-order automatic differentiation.

A dual number \( (v, d) \) represents a value \( v \) and its first derivative \( dv/dx \) with respect to a single variable. All common arithmetic operators and math functions are overloaded to propagate derivatives through the chain rule.
