Detailed Description

DualGraph with a ROOT TGraphErrors mirror for plotting and I/O.

The internal TGraphErrors (graph_) is lazily synced from the DualGraph points via calc(). The is_updated_ flag tracks sync state. Supports construction from TH1 and TGraphErrors, ROOT TFile I/O, and x-axis shifts.

See also
DualGraph (ROOT-independent base), TSystGraph (systematic-uncertainty graphs)
